About ResearchPro

Purpose

ResearchPro was developed to make it easier for aspiring high school students to find research oppurtunities.

How does this Work?

Simply enter the name of the school and the field that you would like to check out for research opportunity, the results will provide information on the professors you can contact

Design

Coded with CSS, HTML, and Python ,leveraged SPACY for NLP capabilities, BING for Search and BeautifulSoup for HTML Processing. Developed by a high school student with a passion for computer science and web design.
