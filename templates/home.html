Purpose

In order to boost their applications, many aspiring high school students want to increase their interest in their field by finding internships with professors at various institutions. Many look through websites such as Indeed, LinkedIN, and others, but no website can offer them the direct matches that they desire. Fortunately, ResearchPro can match those desires. By searching for them, it can save high school students the annoying process of searching for a professor suitable to their needs!
